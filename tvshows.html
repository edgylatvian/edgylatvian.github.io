Parks and Recreation

You have probably watched The Office (American version)
and though that's as far as comedy can go, that there's nothing better.
Well, I have good news for you... Parks and Recreation has everything
that The Office has to offer AND more. Crazy right? The characters are
all different and funny in their own way and throughout the 7 seasons
I guarantee you'll get to love all of them and be sad that the show is over.

with someone else, or you're just an emphatic person. Well, now imagine literally feeling

or rather sharing the bullet with someone else. This phenomenon is extensevly explored in Sense8.
The TV Show is about 8 individuals who have their senses merged together, they can feel each
other when they feel like it, they can visit each other when they feel like it...
they can access knowledge of each other when they feel like it. Check it out bellow.
